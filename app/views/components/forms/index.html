Form control

Form controls inherit the majority of their styling through the .form-control class. By default, form controls are 100% width of their parent. If you require a smaller form control, use the .form-control class alongside the other form control ratio classes, e.g. .form-control-1-2.

Datalist

Much like a select, a datalist input can be used to provide the user with a list of options. A datalist also gives the added functionality of accepting freetext.

Note: the <select> tag is not required within a datalist. It is used here for backward compatibility with older browsers.
